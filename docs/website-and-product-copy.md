# Website And Product Copy

This document provides practical copy starting points for website and product
surfaces.

See also: [Messaging](messaging.md), [Taglines](taglines.md)

## Homepage Hero Candidates

### Option 1

Headline:

`Review. Redact. Share safely.`

Subtext:

"Obsura helps you detect, review, style, and securely remove sensitive
information from screenshots, logs, and technical text."

### Option 2

Headline:

`Modern redaction for screenshots, logs, and technical text.`

Subtext:

"Built for fast, review-first workflows when you need to hide sensitive values
before sharing operational content."

### Option 3

Headline:

`Safe sharing for real technical work.`

Subtext:

"Self-hosted redaction for screenshots, pasted text, logs, and structured
technical content."

## Product Blurb Candidates

- "Obsura is an open-source, self-hosted redaction platform for safely sharing
  screenshots, logs, and technical content."
- "Detect, review, style, and securely remove sensitive information before you
  export."
- "Built for people who need to sanitize technical content quickly without
  losing confidence in the result."

## CTA Guidance

Prefer short, literal calls to action such as:

- `Try Obsura`
- `Review a sample`
- `Redact a screenshot`
- `Run self-hosted`
- `Read the docs`

Avoid vague or inflated calls to action such as:

- `Unlock privacy`
- `Transform your security posture`
- `Experience AI redaction magic`

## Product Copy Standard

Product copy should:

- describe the task clearly
- stay grounded in actual workflows
- sound calm and capable
- avoid pretending the product is broader than it is

If a line sounds like a slogan but does not help the reader understand the
product, replace it with a clearer sentence.
