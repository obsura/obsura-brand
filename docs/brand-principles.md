# Brand Principles

These principles are the practical constraints for future brand work.

See also: [Brand Overview](brand-overview.md),
[Asset Organization](asset-organization.md), [Naming](naming.md)

## 1. Clarity Over Ornament

Brand materials should help people understand the product and use the right
assets. Decorative complexity is not a goal.

## 2. Trust Over Flash

Obsura should feel dependable before it feels impressive. Loud effects, novelty
for its own sake, or aggressive visual signaling weaken that trust.

## 3. Soft Precision Over Hard Security Theater

Obsura should feel exact without becoming cold, brutal, or fear-driven. Prefer
light, calm, highly structured surfaces over dark, aggressive, or threat-themed
presentation.

## 4. Calm Over Hype

Obsura deals with sensitive material. The brand should sound composed,
practical, and explicit about what it does and does not do.

## 5. Practical Identity Over Decorative Identity

The brand exists to support product use, documentation, repo clarity, and safe
reuse across the ecosystem. It is not a standalone art exercise.

## 6. Consistency Across Repositories

Official descriptions, capitalization, and visual usage should stay aligned
across `obsura-*` repositories so contributors do not have to reinterpret the
brand in each repo.

## 7. Open-Source Professionalism

Obsura should feel serious enough for real operational workflows while still
feeling accessible to contributors and self-hosting users.

## 8. Source Of Truth First

If a brand asset or description needs to become official, define it here first.
Downstream reuse should follow the canonical version rather than creating drift.
